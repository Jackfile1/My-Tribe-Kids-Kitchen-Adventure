    • 1 packet instant yeast
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 2/3 cup warm water
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • For the filling:
    • 1 cup tomato sauce
    • 1 cup grated cheese
    • 1 teaspoon Italian herbs

‚ö†Ô∏è Adult needed for:

üåà Rainbow Layer Cake

Advanced 90 mins Vegetarian

You'll need:

  • For the cake:
    • 3 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 cups sugar
    • 3 teaspoons baking powder
    • 4 eggs
    • 1 cup milk
    • 3/4 cup vegetable oil
    • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    • Food coloring (red, yellow, green, blue)
  • For the frosting:
    • 500g cream cheese
    • 2 cups powdered sugar
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

‚ö†Ô∏è Adult needed for:

  • Operating the oven
  • Handling hot pans
  • Using electric mixer
  • Cutting and assembling layers

Steps:

  1. Ask adult to preheat oven to 180¬∞C/350¬∞F
  2. Grease and line cake pans
  3. Mix dry ingredients in large bowl
  4. Mix wet ingredients in separate bowl
  5. Combine wet and dry ingredients
  6. Divide batter into 4 portions
  7. Add different food coloring to each
  8. Bake each layer 20-25 minutes
  9. Cool completely
  10. Make frosting:
    • Beat cream cheese until smooth
    • Add powdered sugar and vanilla
  11. Stack layers with frosting between
  12. Cover entire cake with remaining frosting

Tips:

  • Use gel food coloring for brighter colors
  • Chill layers before frosting
  • Use a serrated knife to level layers

Kitchen Science:

Watch how baking powder makes the cake rise! The heat creates tiny bubbles that make the cake light and fluffy.
